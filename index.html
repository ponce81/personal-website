About me

Originally from Mexico and migrated to Germany around 2006. Software Engineer with a Master of Sciences and a Master in Business Administration (MBA).

I've worked as internal application developer as well as external consultant for several companies in Germany and Switzerland. Focused on SAP Systems.

The last years with SAP going more into cloud solutions I've been able to take a look of what is going on in the cloud ecosystem and find it fascinating. The agility and flexibility that cloud architectures offer and the amount of technologies is just exciting.

Currently I am working on acquiring new skills in the cloud ecosystem. From Cloud Architecture for AWS and Azure, scripting languages like Python to concurrence optimized languages like Go/Golang, mindset of Devops culture with CI/CD Pipelines and Kubernetes, Business Applications with Artificial Intelligence including topics like Ethics of AI and MLOps, starting a journey into Cybersecurity.
